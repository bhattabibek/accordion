10 Days Bhaktapur Tour

Arrival in Kathmandu check in Hotel.

We will pick up from airport and check in Hotel. Overnight at Soaltee Crowne Plaza or similar Evening self-visit tourist place at Pokhara at lake side Overnight at Hotel Pokhara Grande or similar.

After breakfast at Hotel sightseeing in Kathmandu.

Visit Monkey temple, patan durbar squire, pashupatinath , boudhanth stupha. Overnight at Soaltee Crowne Plaza or similar. Get up early in the morning drive to sarankot to see Himalayan view and sunrise view then back to hotel after breakfast at hotel, davis falls, cave, white river and bindabasini temple, world peace pagoda. Overnight at Hotel Pokhara Grande or similar

After breakfast at Hotel drive to chitwan.

Check in Hotel, have a lunch at hotel, then tharu village tour and tharu culture program. Overnight at Barahi jungle lodge meghauli. on the way visit Manokamana self-paying ticket. Overnight at Soaltee Crowne Plaza or similar.

Jungle safari at chitwan.

After breakfast at hotel do cannoning, jungle walking , visit elephant breading center, then elephant safari. Overnight at Barahi jungle lodge meghauli. After breakfast departure to Airport then back to home.
